Dalton's NES Wiki Site Plan

Dalton Broderson

WDD 131

Overview

Purpose

My site will be a sort of a wiki for some of my favorite old Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) games.

Audience

The audience would be other fans retro video games, including fans of the NES and its games.

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements would be the details of each video game. Things like the title, description, release date, developer, publisher, etc. The page would look similar to a wikipedia page, but all of the information will be dynamically loaded.
